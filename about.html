About us

Who We Are

Starlit Distributors is dedicated to providing high-quality lighting and innovative solutions for businesses and homes. We pride ourselves on excellent customer service and a diverse product range.

Background

Founded in the heart of Kingston, Jamaica, Starlit Distributors started with a mission to brighten the lives of our community. Over the years, we have grown to become a trusted supplier of lighting and related products, serving various industries and homes across the region.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to illuminate the world with cutting-edge, eco-friendly lighting solutions while ensuring unmatched customer satisfaction. We aim to be a beacon of excellence in the lighting industry, inspiring innovation and growth in our community.
