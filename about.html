About Us

Who We Are

Starlit Distributors is dedicated to providing high-quality products and reliable solutions for businesses and homes. We pride ourselves on excellent customer service and a diverse product range.

Our Journey

Our genesis is from building and supplying furniture in Kingston environs in 2018. For a short while, we were engaged in supplying gasoline and related products. Those led us to seeing that quality products at reasonable prices plus reliable, customer focus is a rarity. We proceeded to supplying one local prominent hardware electrical wires in 2023. This expanded into supplying a variety of products to government entities. This led to the idea of making it easier for entities and other businesses to be able to source a wide variety of products from one source.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide high-quality products at reasonable prices while ensuring unmatched customer satisfaction. We aim to be a trusted partner for businesses and individuals, offering a seamless sourcing experience for a variety of needs.
